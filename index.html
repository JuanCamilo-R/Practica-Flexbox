Mat fraser

Mathew Fraser (born 1990) is a retired Canadian-American professional CrossFit athlete, competing from 2014 to 2020.
Fraser is the first and only person to win five CrossFit Games titles, winning the 2016,2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020 CrossFit Games consecutively.
He is widely considered to be the most dominant athlete ever in the sport of CrossFit.
